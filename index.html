About us

Name: Stuart Harold Pot (2-D)

Birthday: May 23, 1978

Age: 44

Height: 188cm

Stuart Pot, better known as 2-D, is the main vocalist and frontman for Gorillaz. 2-D is a multi-instrumentalist. He can play both keyboards (piano, synthesizer) and guitar. He also plays the melody.

Name: Noodle (given by band members)

Birthday: October 31, 1990

Age: 32

Height: 157cm

Noodle is the official lead guitarist for Gorillaz. She knew only the word "Noodles" in English, so they began to call her that - Noodle. She soon got used to England, and was accepted into the Gorillaz as the last, fourth member - guitarist.

Name: Murdoc Alphonse (Faust)

Birthday: June 6, 1966

Age: 56

Height: 175cm

Murdoc Niccals is the official bassist, self-proclaimed leader, founder and "king" of the band Gorillaz. Based on young Keith Richards, guitarist of The Rolling Stones, Scooby-Doo Creeper, and Victor Frankenstein.

Name: Russell Hobbs

Birthday: June 3, 1975

Age: 47

Height: 175cm

Russell Hobbs is the official drummer for the Gorillaz. Russell wasn't based on anyone - he was created to epitomize the hip-hop aspect of Gorillaz's music and to embody a collaborative spirit with various rappers.
